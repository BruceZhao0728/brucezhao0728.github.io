Travel Memories | 旅行足迹

⛰️ Gannan | 甘南

Summer 2025 | 2025年夏

Explored the beautiful landscapes of Gannan, including the Labrang Monastery and Zoige Wetland.

探索了甘南的美丽风景，包括拉卜楞寺和若尔盖花湖。

⛩️ Nagoya | 名古屋

Autumn 2024 | 2024年秋

This is my second trip to Japan. I enjoyed seeing the Shinkansen trains in the SC Maglev & Railway Park, and Wagyu beef tasted amazing!

这是我第二次去日本了。这次在磁浮铁道馆看到了新干线列车，还品尝了美味的和牛！

🏜️ Qinghai & Gansu | 青海与甘肃

Summer 2024 | 2024年夏

Traveled through Qinghai and Gansu, visiting Qinghai Lake, Kumbum Monastery, Zhangye Danxia, and the Mogao Grottoes.

闻过青海湖畔的油菜花，摸过塔尔寺的转经筒，走过七彩丹霞的绚烂，赏过莫高窟的壁画。长河落日圆，大漠孤烟直。

🏙️ Guangzhou | 广州

Summer 2023 | 2023年夏

Visited Guangzhou for the first time, exploring the Canton Tower, Shamian Island, Sacred Heart Cathedral, and enjoying delicious Cantonese cuisine!

广州初探：打卡广州塔，邂逅沙面，礼赞圣心大教堂。当然，必不可少的还有那口灵魂烧腩仔。真系正嘢！

🏔️ Yunnan | 云南

Summer 2023 | 2023年夏

The ultimate graduation getaway! Exploring Yunnan with my besties: from the breezy Kunming to the colorful Lijiang, and finally conquering the Jade Dragon Snow Mountain. Best friends, best memories.

毕业盛夏，彩云之南。见证了春城的繁花、丽江的古韵与玉龙的孤傲。此行经年，长留心间。
